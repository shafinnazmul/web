🧠 Tiny design philosophy detour:
Dark themes reduce eye strain in low light but light themes improve readability for dense information tables because the human visual system evolved to detect contrast on bright backgrounds (sunlight on paper). That’s why textbooks, journals, and RFC documents almost always remain light-themed.
